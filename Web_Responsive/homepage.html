Frequently Asked Questions

Question: I'd like to change my ticket.

Answer:
We deal with changeable freight and unchangeable freight.
If you have a changeable freight, you can change it only for the same section and the same airline.
If you wish to change, please contact directly to the airline company you use.
In case of no changeable fare it is necessary to cancel once and apply again.
Question: I want to cancel my ticket.

Answer:
In case of cancellation before payment, there is no need to contact us.
In case you wish to cancel after payment, we will accept cancellation from the special form or by phone.
Please confirm here for details.
Question: What if the airplane is delayed or canceled?

Answer:
In the case of cancellation due to late cancellation, it is possible to withdraw the entire ticket price.
Regarding flight cancellation / delay, it will be judged on the previous day or the same day by the airline.
Please check the flight information from here.
Question: I would like to specify a seat.

Answer:
You can specify your seat at the counter at the airport or you can do it on the airline of each airline in advance. If you specify in advance, we will send you confirmation number.
As soon as we receive your booking number, you can specify your seat if you make a reservation confirmation on each airline HP.
Question: How much is the child fare?

Answer:
The child fare is basically half of the adult normal fare. If the fare for adults is a discount fare, cheap fare amount is applied by comparing the fare amount of the selected fare and the pediatric freight amount.
Regarding the specific amount, please confirm by the breakdown which will be displayed at the time of application.
Question: When has become a pediatric fare?

Answer:
If you are using an airline other than LCC, children aged 3 and over need an infant fare.
For children under 3 years of age, if you do not need a seat (when sitting on the adult's knee accompanying you), one person accompanying you can board one person free of charge.
If you use LCC, children aged 2 and over may have guidance with adults same amount fare.
Children under 2 years old can sit on the lap of one accompanied person for each person, but please be aware that there is a possibility that the fee will be 1,500 yen + handling fee will be charged
